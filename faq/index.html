Which Trailblazer path should I choose?+

Fire for AoE damage in early game, Imaginary for crowd control, or Physical for single-target DPS. Choose based on preference as you can switch later.

Should I pull characters or light cones?+

Pull characters first! Character upgrades matter more than light cones. 4-star light cones are obtainable from shops and battles, while limited characters require reruns.

What CR/CD stats are good enough?+

Standard: 60% CR + 120% CD is passing, 70% + 150% is good, 80% + 180% is excellent. Speed thresholds (134 or 160) are often more important.
