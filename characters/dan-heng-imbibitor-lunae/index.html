Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae

Imaginary Destruction • Single-Target DPS King

💬Discord: dk86968📱Telegram: @rich8696

🎯 Character Overview

Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae (DHIL) is the premier single-target DPS in Honkai: Star Rail. His enhanced basic attacks deal massive multi-hit damage, making him ideal for boss fights and Memory of Chaos.

Key Strengths: Highest single-target damage, excellent against bosses, flexible team building, strong in both MoC and Simulated Universe

⚔️ Light Cone Recommendations

T0 - Signature: "Brighter Than the Sun"

Provides massive CRIT DMG and enhances his basic attack damage. The best option by far, increasing damage by 30-40% compared to 4-star alternatives.

T1 - F2P Options

  • "On the Fall of an Aeon" - Free from events, provides ATK stacking
  • "The Moles Welcome You" - Battle pass option with solid stats
  • "Adversarial" - Good for breaking weaknesses

🛡️ Relic Sets & Stats

💡 Combat Tips

  • Always use enhanced basic attack (3 stacks) for maximum damage
  • Save ultimate for bosses or when Bronya buff is active
  • Prioritize breaking weaknesses before dealing damage
  • In MoC, focus fire on one target to eliminate quickly
