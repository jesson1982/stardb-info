Character Overview

Blade is a 5-star Wind Destruction character who scales with both CRIT stats and Max HP. His unique "Shards Sword" mechanic allows him to enhance basic attacks after taking damage or healing, making him a powerful sustained DPS.

Key Strengths

  • ✅ High CRIT DMG scaling
  • ✅ Enhanced Basic Attack AoE damage
  • ✅ Self-sustain through HP sacrifice
  • ✅ Strong follow-up attack potential

Best Light Cones

Light Cone Rarity Effect Rating
The Unreachable Side ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Increases CRIT Rate by 18%. When HP decreases or is healed, increases DMG by 40% (stacks up to 2 times) S-Tier

Naught Is Sweet ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Increases CRIT Rate by 12%. When HP is above 80%, increases Basic and Skill DMG by 40% A-Tier

Relic Sets & Stats

Recommended Set: Hunter + Glamoth

  • 4-Piece Hunter: Increases Wind DMG by 10%. After using Ultimate, CRIT Rate increases by 25% for 2 turns
  • 2-Piece Glamoth: Increases ATK by 12%. For every 100 SPD above 120, increases DMG by 8% (max 32%)

Main Stats Priority:

  1. Body: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (aim for 1:2 ratio)
  2. Feet: SPD
  3. Planar Sphere: Wind DMG Bonus
  4. Link Rope: ATK% or Energy Regeneration Rate

Substats Priority:

CRIT Rate/DMG > SPD > ATK% > HP%

Stat Target: Aim for 70% CRIT Rate / 140% CRIT DMG minimum. With signature light cone, you can reach 80%/160% easily.

Team Compositions

Hypercarry Team

Blade + Bronya + Tingyun + Luocha

Bronya provides extra turns and CRIT DMG buff. Tingyun gives energy and ATK boost. Luocha keeps Blade healthy while enabling his self-damage mechanics.

DoT Synergy Team

Blade + Kafka + Ruan Mei + Huohuo

Kafka triggers DoT effects including Blade's "Strife" stacks. Ruan Mei provides break efficiency and DMG bonus. Huohuo sustains the team.

Eidolon Priority

Eidolon Effect Priority
E1 When using enhanced Basic Attack, ignores 20% of enemy DEF High
E2 Using Ultimate reduces Wind RES by 20% for 2 turns Medium
E4 Enhanced Basic Attack hits all enemies and deals additional DMG equal to 15% of Blade's lost HP High
E6 When HP drops below 50%, immediately restores 20% HP and gains 1 stack of Shards Sword (once per turn) Low

Optimal Rotation

Basic Combo Pattern

  1. Step 1: Use Skill to consume HP and gain Shards Sword stacks
  2. Step 2: Use enhanced Basic Attack (deals massive AoE Wind DMG)
  3. Step 3: Use Ultimate when available (cleanses debuffs + heals)
  4. Step 4: Repeat Skill → Enhanced BA rotation

Advanced Tips

  • Maintain HP around 50-70% to maximize damage from light cone passives
  • Use Ultimate strategically to cleanse important debuffs in MoC
  • Coordinate with healer timing to trigger Shards Sword efficiently
