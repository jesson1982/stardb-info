🛡️ Preservation Path (Fire)

The Fire Trailblazer serves as an excellent shielder and taunt tank, providing strong defensive utility for the team.

Recommended Build

  • Light Cone: Trend of the Universal Market (4★) or Day One of My New Life (3★)
  • Relic Set: 4-Piece Knight + 2-Piece Guard
  • Main Stats: DEF% Body, SPD Feet, Fire DMG Sphere, Energy Rope
  • Substats: DEF% > Effect RES > SPD > HP%

Key Abilities

  • Skill: Provides massive shield based on Max HP
  • Ultimate: Taunts all enemies and increases DEF
  • Talent: Counter-attacks when hit (Fire DMG)

Best Teams

Hypercarry Protection: DPS + Support + TB (Preservation) + Flex

Perfect for protecting squishy DPS like Seele or Jing Yuan in Memory of Chaos.

⚔️ Destruction Path (Physical)

The Physical Trailblazer offers solid single-target damage with good break efficiency, making them a reliable early-game DPS option.

Recommended Build

  • Light Cone: On the Fall of an Aeon (4★) or The Moles Welcome You (3★)
  • Relic Set: 4-Piece Champion + 2-Piece Musketeer
  • Main Stats: CRIT Body, ATK% Feet, Physical DMG Sphere, ATK% Rope
  • Substats: CRIT Rate/DMG > ATK% > SPD > Break Effect

Key Abilities

  • Skill: Deals Physical DMG and reduces enemy DEF
  • Ultimate: Massive single-target Physical DMG
  • Talent: Additional DMG against broken enemies

✨ Harmony Path (Imaginary)

The Imaginary Trailblazer provides unique support capabilities with skill point generation and DMG buffs, excelling in turn-based strategies.

Recommended Build

  • Light Cone: Dance! Dance! Dance! (4★) or Meshing Cogs (3★)
  • Relic Set: 4-Piece Messenger + 2-Piece Fleet
  • Main Stats: HP% or DEF% Body, SPD Feet, Imaginary DMG Sphere, Energy Rope
  • Substats: SPD > Effect Hit Rate > HP% > DEF%

Key Abilities

  • Skill: Buffs ally ATK and enables follow-up attacks
  • Ultimate: Advances forward ally's action and regenerates SP
  • Talent: Generates extra Skill Points

Best Teams

Follow-Up Team: Clara/March 7th + TB (Harmony) + Tingyun + Healer

Amplifies follow-up attack characters while maintaining skill point economy.

When to Use Each Path

1

Use Preservation: When your team needs protection, especially in high-damage MoC phases or against elite bosses.

2

Use Destruction: In early game before you have 5-star DPS, or for breaking Physical weakness enemies.

3

Use Harmony: In follow-up attack teams or when you need extra skill points for skill-heavy compositions.

4

Investment Priority: Preservation > Harmony > Destruction for endgame content viability.
