Character Overview

Bailu is a 5-star Lightning Abundance character who serves as one of the strongest healers in Honkai: Star Rail. Her unique ability to revive fallen allies makes her invaluable in challenging content like Memory of Chaos.

Key Strengths

  • ✅ Massive AoE healing with Skill
  • ✅ Can revive dead allies (Ultimate)
  • ✅ Self-sustain with passive healing
  • ✅ Energy regeneration support

Relic Sets & Stats

Recommended Set: Passerby + Fleet

  • 4-Piece Passerby: Increases Outgoing Healing by 10%. At start of battle, immediately regenerates 1 Skill Point
  • 2-Piece Fleet: Increases Outgoing Healing by 10%

Main Stats Priority:

  1. Body: Outgoing Healing Boost
  2. Feet: HP% or SPD
  3. Planar Sphere: HP%
  4. Link Rope: Energy Regeneration Rate (min 120%)

Substats Priority:

HP% > SPD > Effect RES > DEF%

Team Compositions

Hypercarry Sustain Team

DPS + Support + Bailu + Flex

Bailu provides consistent healing and energy regen for hypercarry teams. Works well with Seele, Jing Yuan, or any single-target DPS.

DoT Team Healer

Kafka + Black Swan + Bailu + Ruan Mei

In DoT teams, Bailu keeps everyone alive while DoT effects deal damage. Her revives are clutch against high-damage bosses.

Eidolon Priority

Eidolon Effect Priority
E1 When Bailu heals an ally with less than 50% HP, Outgoing Healing increases by 15% High
E2 Revived allies have their HP restored to 100% Medium
E4 Using Ultimate increases all allies' DMG by 15% for 2 turns High
E6 When an ally's HP drops below 35%, immediately heals them for 50% of Bailu's Max HP (once per ally per battle) Low

Combat Tips

1

Energy Management: Aim for 120%+ ER to use Ultimate every rotation. This ensures revives are always available.

2

Skill Timing: Use Skill before big enemy attacks to pre-shield your team with HoT effects.

3

Revive Strategy: Save Ultimate for emergency revives rather than using it on cooldown.

4

Positioning: Place Bailu in teams where she can benefit from energy recharge supports like Tingyun.
