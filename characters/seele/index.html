Character Overview

Seele is a 5-star Quantum Hunt character specializing in single-target burst damage. Her signature "Resurgence" talent allows her to gain extra turns after defeating enemies, making her exceptional at clearing waves quickly.

Key Strengths

  • ✅ Extra turn mechanic (Resurgence)
  • ✅ High single-target burst damage
  • ✅ Quantum weakness implant synergy
  • ✅ Excellent wave-clearing potential

Best Light Cones

Light Cone Rarity Effect Rating
Cruising in the Stellar Sea ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Increases CRIT Rate by 18%. When attacking enemies with lower than 50% HP, increases DMG by 40% S-Tier

Relic Sets & Stats

Recommended Set: Genius + Rutilant

  • 4-Piece Genius: Increases Quantum DMG by 10%. When attacking enemies, ignores 10% DEF. If target has Quantum Weakness, additional 10% DEF ignore
  • 2-Piece Rutilant: Increases ATK by 12%. When SPD is above 120, increases Basic Attack and Skill DMG by 12%

Main Stats Priority:

  1. Body: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG
  2. Feet: SPD (aim for 134+ threshold)
  3. Planar Sphere: Quantum DMG Bonus
  4. Link Rope: ATK%

Substats Priority:

CRIT Rate/DMG > SPD > ATK% > Effect Hit Rate

SPD Breakpoint: Aim for 134 SPD to act twice in first rotation with proper support buffs. This maximizes Resurgence potential.

Team Compositions

Quantum Mono Team

Seele + Silver Wolf + Bronya/Tingyun + Luocha/Bailu

Silver Wolf implants Quantum weakness on any enemy, enabling Seele's full damage potential. Bronya provides extra turns to chain Resurgences.

Speed Tuned Team

Seele + Pela + Asta + Gepard

Pela reduces enemy DEF and applies Aether Editing debuff. Asta provides massive SPD buff to help Seele reach breakpoints. Gepard offers freeze control.

Eidolon Priority

Eidolon Effect Priority
E1 When using Resurgence, Seele's DMG increases by 50% for the extra turn High
E2 Using Ultimate reduces Quantum RES by 20% for 2 turns Medium
E4 After defeating an enemy, regenerates 15 Energy immediately High
E6 Ultimate can trigger Resurgence even if it doesn't defeat the target (deals 100% of Seele's ATK as additional Quantum DMG) Low

Advanced Rotation

Optimal Combo Sequence

  1. Step 1: Use Skill on weakest enemy (to trigger Resurgence)
  2. Step 2: If Resurgence triggers, use another Skill on next target
  3. Step 3: Use Ultimate when energy is full or for burst phases
  4. Step 4: Repeat Skill → Resurgence chain until all enemies cleared

Wave Management Tips

  • Always target lowest HP enemies first to maximize Resurgence chains
  • Save Ultimate for elite enemies or boss phases
  • Coordinate with supports to ensure you act first in each wave
  • In MoC, prioritize killing adds before focusing the main boss
