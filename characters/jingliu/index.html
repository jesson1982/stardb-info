🎯 Character Overview

Jingliu, the former Sword Champion of the Cloud Knights, is an Ice Destruction character with a unique "Transcendence" mechanic. By consuming ally HP to enter "Lunar Embodiment" state, she gains massive CRIT DMG bonus and unlocks enhanced skills, becoming one of the strongest single-target burst DPS in the current meta.

Key Strengths: Extremely high CRIT DMG scaling, excellent single-target damage, relatively forgiving relic requirements (built-in CRIT DMG bonus)
Important Notes: Requires sufficient healing support in team, Lunar Embodiment has limited duration, need to plan rotation carefully

T1 - Four-Star Alternatives

  • "Something Irreplaceable" - General Hunt LC, healing on kill suitable for sustained fights
  • "Swordplay" - Single-target specialized, significant damage when fully stacked, ideal for Memory of Chaos
  • "Cruising in the Stellar Sea" - Nameless Glory LC, CRIT Rate + ATK buff on kill

👥 Team Compositions

Composition 1: Classic Triple-Support (Recommended)

Team Setup

Jingliu (Main DPS)
Bronya (Action Advance + DMG Buff)
Pela (DEF Shred Support)
Bailu/Fu Xuan (Sustain)

Core Strategy: Bronya advances Jingliu's action for consecutive turns, Pela's Ultimate applies group DEF reduction, maximizing single-burst damage. Ideal for quick clears in Memory of Chaos.

Composition 2: Dual-Carry Balanced

Team Setup

Jingliu
Seele/Blade
Asta/Tingyun
Natasha/Trailblazer (Fire)

Core Strategy: Handle multiple waves with two DPS characters alternating attacks to avoid damage downtime. Suitable for long Simulated Universe runs.

🔄 Optimal Rotation & Combat Tips

Jingliu's standard rotation: Basic Attack to build Transcendence points → Skill to enter Lunar Embodiment → Enhanced Basic Attack/Skill damage → Ultimate burst.

Key Technique: Use Ultimate + Enhanced Skill combination in the final turn of Lunar Embodiment state for maximum burst damage. Monitor ally HP carefully to avoid accidental deaths.
