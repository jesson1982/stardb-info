Kafka Build Guide

Lightning Nihility • DoT System Core

💬Discord: dk86968📱Telegram: @rich8696

🎯 Character Overview

Kafka is the core of DoT (Damage over Time) teams, specializing in detonating ongoing damage effects. Her skill triggers all DoT on enemies, making her essential for Black Swan, Sampo, and Guinaifen compositions.

Key Strengths: Excellent DoT detonation, good AoE coverage, flexible team building, strong in both single-target and multi-wave content

Stat Thresholds

Effect Hit Rate: Aim for 80-100% to ensure consistent DoT application. Beyond 100% has diminishing returns.
Speed: 134 SPD breakpoint is ideal for optimal turn frequency.
ATK: Balance with Effect Hit Rate; don't sacrifice too much ATK for EHR.

👥 Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Classic DoT Team

Kafka (DoT Detonator)
Black Swan (DoT Dealer)
Guinaifen (Fire DoT + DEF Shred)
Huohuo/Bailu (Sustain)

Strategy: Apply multiple DoTs, then use Kafka's skill to detonate all at once. Huohuo provides buffs and healing.

Team 2: Wind DoT Variant

Kafka
Sampo (Wind DoT)
Silver Wolf (DEF Reduction)
Luocha (Healing)

Strategy: Silver Wolf applies weakness, Sampo stacks wind shear, Kafka detonates for massive burst.

🔄 Combat Rotation

Standard rotation: Skill (apply shock) → Basic Attack → Ultimate (AoE shock) → Skill (detonate). Against DoT-heavy teams, prioritize using skill after allies apply their DoTs.

Pro Tip: Save ultimate for waves with multiple enemies to maximize AoE shock application. Use skill immediately after Black Swan's arcana explosion for maximum damage.
